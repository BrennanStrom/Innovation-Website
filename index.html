The Keystone Analog Mechanical Keyboard (KAMK) utilizes AI Technology to make keying comfortable.
Say your a Paralegal like my mother you need to type a lot of cases and they can take a lot of time, but with the KAMK you can key even faster.
Depending on how hard you press the keys it will capitalize or lowercase also it tracks the data on how hard you press the keys.
Then after a while it will make some keys easier to press and some keys harder to press for easy typing.
